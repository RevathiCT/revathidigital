SEO

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is a collection of methods, tactics, and practices aimed at increasing a website’s or web page’s exposure and ranking in search engine results pages (SERPs)

SMM

Social media marketing is a digital marketing approach that involves promoting products, services, brands, or information to a target audience using social media networks

SEM

Search Engine Marketing (SEM) is a digital marketing technique that involves raising the visibility of a website or web page in search engine results pages (SERPs) through paid advertising.

Affiliated marketing

Affiliate marketing is a marketing approach that entails collaborations between a company and external affiliates in order to promote products or services. These affiliates are compensated for increasing traffic or creating sales, leads, or other desirable business actions.
